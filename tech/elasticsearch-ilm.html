Elasticsearch: ILM for Time-Series Indices

ILM turns “keep logs forever” into “keep what we need, where we need it, for as long as we need it.”

The model

  • Rollover: new backing indices by size/age/doc-count
  • Tiers: hot → warm → cold → frozen
  • Delete: retention enforcement

Why rollover matters

Big shards are slow to move and slow to recover. Rollover keeps shard sizes predictable.
