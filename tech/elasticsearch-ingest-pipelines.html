Elasticsearch: Ingest Pipelines for Clean Data

Ingest pipelines transform documents as they arrive—ETL at the edge.

Common processors

  • grok (parse logs)
  • date (normalize timestamps)
  • rename/set/remove (cleanup)
  • geoip (enrichment)

Design tips

  • Prefer small composable pipelines
  • Add failure handling so a single bad event doesn’t poison ingestion
  • Version pipelines alongside index templates
