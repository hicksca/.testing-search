Proxmox: Backup, Restore, and PBS Integration

Snapshots are rollback tools. Backups are survival tools.

vzdump is the built-in workhorse

You can run backups online (snapshot mode) or offline for maximum consistency. The goal is consistent, repeatable restores.

PBS adds the “grown up” features

  • Deduplication (especially great across many similar VMs)
  • Verification
  • Encryption
  • Retention policies that are easier to live with

Restore strategy that actually works

  • Restore one VM monthly as a canary
  • Document restore order and dependencies
  • Test both full restore and file-level recovery
