Elasticsearch: Snapshots and Disaster Recovery

Snapshots are the only supported “real backup” mechanism in Elasticsearch. They’re incremental at the segment level, so repeated snapshots are efficient.

SLM (Snapshot Lifecycle Management)

SLM schedules snapshots and retention. A failing SLM policy is a quiet disaster waiting to happen.

Restore planning

  • Scope: full cluster vs selective indices
  • RTO expectations
  • Metadata dependencies: templates, ILM, pipelines, security config
