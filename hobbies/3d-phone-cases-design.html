Designing for Real-World Fit

Phone cases look easy until you print one. Fit comes down to tolerances, button cutouts, and corner strength.

Measurements that matter

  • Overall width/height/thickness (including camera bump)
  • Button center points + clearance for travel
  • Port cutouts (connector + cable clearance)

Tolerances

A good starting point is +0.2mm to +0.5mm clearance depending on material and printer accuracy.
