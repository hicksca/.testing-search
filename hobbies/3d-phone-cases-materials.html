PLA vs PETG vs TPU

Material choice decides feel, durability, and how annoying the print will be.

  • PLA: easy and pretty, but can crack and soften in heat
  • PETG: tougher, slightly flexible, good general option
  • TPU: best protection and grip, but needs slower printing and tuning

If you want “daily driver” protection, TPU wins—if you’re willing to tune for it.
